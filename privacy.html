1. Who We Are

OneCallShield LLC ("OneCallShield," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website onecallshield.com and provides an insurance lead matching service that connects consumers seeking insurance quotes with licensed insurance agents.

This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected through our website, services, and any related communications.

Our Promise: We connect you with ONE licensed insurance agent. Your information is never sold to multiple agents, insurance companies, or third-party marketing lists.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you submit a quote request through our website, we collect:

  • Personal identifiers: First name, last name, email address, phone number
  • Demographic information: Age, ZIP code
  • Insurance information: Type of insurance requested, coverage preferences, health status (for life insurance)
  • Contact preferences: Preferred call time
  • Consent records: Date, time, and IP address of your TCPA consent

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referring website or advertisement

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Matching services: To match you with one licensed insurance agent best suited to your needs
  • Communications: To send you confirmation emails and status updates about your request
  • Service improvement: To improve our matching process and website experience
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
  • Fraud prevention: To detect and prevent fraudulent activity

We do not: Sell your personal information to data brokers, marketing companies, or multiple insurance agents. Your information is shared only with the single agent we match you with.

4. How We Share Your Information

With Your Matched Agent

When we match you with a licensed insurance agent, we share your contact information and insurance request details exclusively with that one agent. This is the core purpose of our service.

Service Providers

We work with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business:

  • Email delivery: Resend (for sending confirmation and notification emails)
  • Database: Google Firebase (for secure data storage)
  • Hosting: Cloudflare (for website hosting and security)

These providers are contractually required to protect your information and may only use it to provide services to us.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of OneCallShield, our users, or the public.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email if your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5. TCPA Consent & Communications

By submitting your information through our website, you expressly consent to receive:

  • Calls, texts, and/or emails from one licensed insurance agent matched to your request
  • Email communications related to your insurance quote request
  • Follow-up contact from your matched agent via phone or text message

Important: Your consent is provided under the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA). You are consenting to be contacted by one agent only — not multiple agents or insurance companies. Standard message and data rates may apply for text communications.

Opting Out

You may opt out of communications at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any text message
  • Clicking the unsubscribe link in any email
  • Contacting us directly at [email protected]

6. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure cloud database storage via Google Firebase
  • Access controls limiting who can view your information
  • Regular security monitoring

While we implement these safeguards, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions
  • Opt-out: Opt out of communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected]. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To submit a CCPA request, contact us at [email protected].

8. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience. These include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Session storage: Used to maintain your login session on the admin and agent portals

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
