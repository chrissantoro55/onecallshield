Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How does OneCallShield actually work?
You fill out one quick form with your insurance needs. Our team reviews your request and hand-selects exactly one licensed, vetted agent from our network who specializes in what you need. We send your info only to them — and they reach out once to discuss your options. That's it.
Is OneCallShield really free for me?
Yes, 100% free for consumers. Insurance agents pay us for exclusive access to your lead. You never pay anything — our business model ensures your interests and ours are aligned.
Will my information be shared with multiple agents?
Absolutely not. Your information is shared with exactly one agent, period. This is our core promise and the entire reason we exist. We will never sell or distribute your data to multiple buyers.
What types of insurance do you cover?
We cover Auto, Homeowners, Renters, Life (term and whole), Health, Medicare, and other specialty insurance types. If you're not sure, just select "Other" in the form and describe what you need.
How are agents vetted?
Every agent in our network is verified as actively licensed in their state, reviewed for professional standing, and agrees to our conduct standards — including a commitment to contact you only once unless you request otherwise.
What does TCPA compliant mean?
The Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) sets rules around how businesses can contact consumers by phone. Being TCPA compliant means we collect proper consent from you before any agent calls, protecting your rights and ensuring everything is done legally and transparently.
How much do leads cost?
We charge per lead based on insurance type: Auto $25 · Homeowners $35 · Renters $20 · Life $50 · Health $45 · Medicare $50. During our beta launch, your first month of leads is completely free so you can evaluate quality before committing.
When am I charged for a lead?
You are never charged until a lead is actually delivered to you. Your card is saved securely when you sign up but nothing is billed until we match and send you a consumer. No lead, no charge — guaranteed.
How quickly will I receive leads?
Once your application is approved and your account is active, leads are matched and sent to you typically within hours of a consumer submitting. You'll receive an email notification immediately with the consumer's full details.
Are leads truly exclusive?
100% exclusive. Each consumer is matched with exactly one agent — you. We never sell the same lead to multiple agents. This is the entire foundation of OneCallShield and something we take very seriously.
What states and insurance types do you cover?
We operate nationwide across all 50 states. We cover Auto, Homeowners, Renters, Life, Health, Medicare, and other specialty types. You'll only receive leads for the states and insurance types you're licensed for.
What happens if a lead is bad quality?
We stand behind our leads. If a consumer submitted incorrect information or is completely unreachable after multiple attempts, contact us and we'll review it. We want our agents to succeed — your success is our success.
Do I get my own portal to manage leads?
Yes — every approved agent gets access to a dedicated agent portal at onecallshield.com/portal. You can view all your leads, add call notes, set callback reminders, update your status, and manage your licensed states and insurance types — all in one place.
How do I get approved?
Simply fill out our agent application at onecallshield.com/agents. We review all applications within 24 hours. We verify your license, review your experience, and if everything checks out we'll send you your portal login credentials and you're ready to receive leads.
