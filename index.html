Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How does OneCallShield actually work?
You fill out one quick form with your insurance needs. Our team reviews your request and hand-selects exactly one licensed, vetted agent from our network who specializes in what you need. We send your info only to them — and they reach out once to discuss your options. That's it.
Is OneCallShield really free for me?
Yes, 100% free for consumers. Insurance agents pay us for exclusive access to your lead. You never pay anything — our business model ensures your interests and ours are aligned.
Will my information be shared with multiple agents?
Absolutely not. Your information is shared with exactly one agent, period. This is our core promise and the entire reason we exist. We will never sell or distribute your data to multiple buyers.
What types of insurance do you cover?
We cover Auto, Homeowners, Renters, Life (term and whole), Health, Medicare, and other specialty insurance types. If you're not sure, just select "Other" in the form and describe what you need.
How are agents vetted?
Every agent in our network is verified as actively licensed in their state, reviewed for professional standing, and agrees to our conduct standards — including a commitment to contact you only once unless you request otherwise.
What does TCPA compliant mean?
The Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) sets rules around how businesses can contact consumers by phone. Being TCPA compliant means we collect proper consent from you before any agent calls, protecting your rights and ensuring everything is done legally and transparently.
